# Force & motion

## Newton's laws of motion

1. if $F = 0$ , then $v_i = v_f$
2. $F = ma$
3. $F_{ab} = -F_{ba}$

## Pulley

**Fixed pulley**
$$T = w$$

$$F_{fix} = 2T$$
**Movable pulley**
$$T = \frac w2$$

$$F_{fix}= T$$

## Circular motion

**Horizontal**
$$F = ma = m\frac{v^2}{r}$$
**Vertical**
$$v_{top} = \sqrt{gr}$$

$$v_{mid} = \sqrt{3gr}$$

$$v_{bottom} = \sqrt{5gr}$$

## Spring

$$F = k\Delta x$$

$$mg = k\Delta x$$

consider spring mass $m_s$

$$x = x_i+\frac{mg}{k}+\frac{m_sg}{2k}$$
