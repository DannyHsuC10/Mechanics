# Electricity
## Voltage
$$V = IR$$

$$V  = \frac{E}{Q}$$
## Current
$$I = \frac{Q}{t}$$

## Resistance
$$R = \frac{V}{I}$$

$$R = \frac{Pl}{A}$$

$P =$ resistivity, unit : $\Omega\cdot m$
$l =$ length
$A =$ area

## Energy
$$E = QV = IVt$$

## Power
$$P = \frac{E}{t} = IV = I^2R = \frac{V^2}{R}$$

## Series and parallel circuits


|         |            Series             |           parallel            |
|:-------:|:-----------------------------:|:-----------------------------:|
| Voltage |   $$V_{tt} = V_1+V_2...+V_n$$   | $$V_{tt} = V_1 = V_2... = V_n$$ |
| Current | $$I_{tt} = I_1 = I_2... = I_n$$ |   $$I_{tt} = I_1+I_2...+I_n$$   |
